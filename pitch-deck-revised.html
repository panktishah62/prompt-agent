What
An AI that searches Amazon, Flipkart AND calls local shops near you to find the actual cheapest price.
PriceHunter is not just another comparison layer. It combines online marketplace search with a voice agent that calls local shops, negotiates for the user, and returns the final ranked answer.
01

You ask like a human

“Cheapest iPhone 15 near Koramangala” and that is enough. Just type what you want. No filters, no categories, no browsing.

02

AI understands everything

Product, location, budget intent are parsed instantly. It knows whether you want the cheapest, closest, or fastest option.

03

Online prices searched

Amazon, Flipkart, Croma, Blinkit, BigBasket and other relevant platforms are queried simultaneously in seconds.

04

Local shops called by AI

This is the part no one else does. An AI voice agent phones nearby vendors, asks for live price, negotiates a discount, and speaks in Hindi or English.

05

One ranked answer

Online and offline results are scored by price, delivery, and availability. The best deal wins whether it is Amazon or the shop around the corner.
